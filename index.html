About Me

An Introduction

As a Software Developer I strive to bring quality soft products, services and support to the software industry so that customers and end users alike have a smooth and painless experience while using them.

When I began in the software industry, I started out as a game tester with Nintendo of America. Over the years I performed various rolls within the testing department and the experience taught me a lot about the development cycle with an emphasis on debugging software. Though it was a great experience, deep down I wanted to be making software, not trying break it.

With the goal of becoming a developer in mind I taught myself C#, a little HTML and a little CSS. I put those skills to work making games and tools for myself with the Unity Engine. Over the course of a total of four years, I'd made some progress and had a handful of various projects under my belt. I dabbled in programming, VR, 3D modeling, rigging, animation, texturing and sound design.

Slowly, these experiences revealed to me that though developing games was fun, the world of code was where I really wanted to be regardless of the project. So, I adjusted my path to match my passion and I enrolled in Code Fellows, a coding bootcamp. With class courses underway, I currently purse the dream of being a software developer working professionally in the software industry.
